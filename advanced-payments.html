Frequently Asked Questions

What’s an add-on in FreshBooks? How is it charged to my account?

An add-on is a feature you can selectively add or remove from your account that provides functionality that’s not available with regular FreshBooks’ plans. The Advanced Payments add-on can be selected through the Billing & Upgrade page in your account or directly at this link. All add-ons are charged to your account monthly.

Should I have used online payments in the past in order to understand and use Advanced Payments?

You do not need to have used online payments before! However, you will need to complete the set-up of your FreshBooks Payments account or connect your Stripe account to FreshBooks before you see the option to add Advanced Payments to your account. Review the instructions for completing your FreshBooks Payments account set-up here.

Does this mean recurring invoices have an add-on charge too?

Nope! Recurring invoices are still available for free in all FreshBooks plans and you can even add the option for clients to pay you with a credit card for free as well. The additional functionality offered with Advanced Payments is the ability to require a credit card on the recurring invoice, so your client must choose this method of payment instead of other available options. Once they add their card, they will also be automatically charged every month, similar to a subscription-service. 

But I already save client credit card info on a paper/in my computer/in my phone and send invoices to myself, why should I pay for it?

This is not a safe way to store client data, let alone credit card data. There are regulations in the credit card industry that most companies choose to follow because it is the safe way to store client card data (this is called PCI Compliance). Advanced Payments (and all of New FreshBooks) is PCI Compliant, so you know your client data is completely safe and secure. If you choose not to safely store credit card data, you risk being charged if the information is compromised.

Why is my rate higher?

Advanced Payments charges 3.5% + 30¢/transaction (rates will vary depending on region) for all credit cards processed through it, which is higher than the rate for most cards regularly processed with FreshBooks Payments. This is a standard pattern for all keyed-in card transactions across most providers because these types of charges are a higher fraud risk.

Why can’t I see Advanced Payments in my account? I want to get it!

If you haven’t completed setting up your FreshBooks Payments account or connected your Stripe account to FreshBooks, you won’t be able to see the option to purchase Advanced Payments. You automatically have a FreshBooks Payments account when you sign-up for FreshBooks, however you will need to answer a few questions and connect your bank in order to be fully set-up. Review the instructions for completing your FreshBooks Payments account set-up here.
