Frequently Asked Questions

Can my clients access their estimates online?

Since FreshBooks is cloud based, your clients can access your estimates online whenever it is convenient to them.

Can I easily turn my estimates into an invoice?

Yup. With FreshBooks, you can easily convert your estimates into an invoice to save you time and make billing painless.

Are my estimates safe in the cloud?

The safety of your private data is our top priority, that’s why it’s protected by 256-bit SSL encryption—the gold standard in internet security. FreshBooks is cloud based and uses industry-leading secure servers.

Are FreshBooks estimates professional looking?

Absolutely. Plus you can customize the look and feel of your estimates to match your brand and business, including adjusting the estimates template, font, color and imagery.
